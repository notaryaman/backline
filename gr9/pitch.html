Just-in-time support

When a retail pick goes sideways, newer workers need recovery support fast.

Backline is a shift support tool for missing items, contextual escalation, and interruption recovery under timer pressure.

Problem

Training explains official workflow, but not what to do when the item is missing and the timer is still running.

Insight

The hardest part of the job is not picking. It is recovering when uncertainty breaks the plan.

Solution

Backline gives workers the fastest safe next step instead of forcing guesswork or unnecessary escalation.

01

Alternate locations, substitute guidance, and unavailable-item fallback.

02

One-tap requests that package aisle, issue, and timer state.

03
