Backline

Just-in-time support for retail fulfillment workers during missing items, escalation moments, and customer interruptions.

GR9 Poster

Aryaman Singh and Kushagra Kanaujia

University of California, Santa Barbara

Key takeaway

The hardest part of retail fulfillment is not picking items. It is recovering when the plan breaks.

Problem

Breakdown moments create hidden work.

Formal training explains scanner mechanics and official workflow, but newer workers still lose time and confidence when an item is missing, when a substitute is risky, or when a customer interruption wipes out task context.
