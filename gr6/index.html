GR6 Medium-Fi Prototype

Guided recovery flows for missing items, interruptions, and escalation.

Backline is a medium-fidelity prototype for retail fulfillment associates working under timer pressure. This version reflects the GR6 brief: simple, moderate, and complex tasks, revised interface changes from low-fi, and explicit prototype limitations.

Usability goals

  • Reduce hesitation during uncertainty-heavy moments.
  • Make help-seeking feel fast and competent.

Measured in testing

  • Time to choose a recovery action
  • Navigation errors or dead ends

Major revisions from low-fi

Guidance and action now live on the same screen.
A persistent task context header survives interruptions.
Help requests are structured escalation cards with auto-filled context.
